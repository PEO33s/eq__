Earthquake Safety

Indoors

During an earthquake, drop, cover, and hold on. Avoid windows, heavy furniture, and outside walls. Don't panic, don't run outside, and don't use elevators.

Outdoors

Move to an open, safe area away from hazards like buildings and power lines.

Driving

Pull over to a clear spot and stay in the car.

Trapped

Don't light matches. Cover your mouth and tap to signal for help.
